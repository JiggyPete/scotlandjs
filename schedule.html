LiveJS: Web audio + visual spectacular

What happens when two people who’ve never met before share a common interest in live visualizations? #1: They founded a group dedicated to Web VJ. #2: They decided to do a talk on how to combine Web Audio, MIDI, Animation & Sockets to create an interactive audio + visual performance.
