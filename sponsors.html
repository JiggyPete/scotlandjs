Diversity Support

ScotlandJS has received a great deal of positive feedback about how warm, welcoming and friendly our event is.

For 2016 we want to go even further, and are explicitly focusing on setting a new standard of inclusivity.

We want to be accessible and supportive to people from underrepresented groups, building their trust and encouraging their participation within the community.

To achieve this we need your support, which will allow us to:
  1. offer travel and accommodation support to speakers from underrepresented groups.
  2. offer 30 free tickets to each event to people from underrepresented groups.
  3. offer scholarships (financial assistance with travel and accommodation) for up to 5 attendees who would not be able to attend otherwise.
  4. offer free childcare to attendees.

We have divided these items so you can support our efforts in a way that makes sense for your company, be that supporting a single (or many) speakers, scholarships or attendees, or simply make a donation.

In return, we will put your company's logo in the Supporter section of this website and let everyone know of you are helping us establish a stronger, more diverse and inclusive community!

Support options

Support a speaker(s) from an underrepresented group: £500
Fund a ticket(s) for people from an underrepresented group: £240
Fund a schollarship(s) for people from an underrepresented group: £600

Thank you for taking the time to find out what we are trying to achieve and if you would like to assist with any of the suport options please contact peter@scotlandjs.com.
