ScotlandJS is coming soon!

We've got a lot of exciting news to tell you about ScotlandJS!

First, we'll be closing childcare places on Friday 22nd June. This is so we can ensure that there are enough staff in place to take care of your children. If you haven't been in touch about making use of the childcare service please get in touch before Friday.

Plan for the big event:

Both day 1 and 2 will follow the same format, with the exception of day 2 starting a little later.

8:00am: Open for Registration, tea and coffee will be provided

9:00am: Conference talks begin

11:30am: Break

1:20pm: Lunch

4:00pm: Break

6:00pm: Close

Have dinner at a venue of your choice (not included)

Day 1 7:30pm: Choice of Board Games or Whisky & Gin Tasting. No need to sign up for Board Games, however Whisky & Gin Tasting space is limited. Sign up here to avoid disappointment.

Day 2: 7:30pm: After-party at Hemma

Supporting future speakers!

Having launched Global Diversity CFP Day we have made a huge commitment to encouraging new speakers to take to the stage.

During ScotlandJS we want to give a number of you the opporuntity to be onstage without the stress of having to put together a talk.

You won't have to do anything except be onstage. The speakers won't ask you questions or to do anything. Simply sit at the other side of the stage from the speaker, ideally with a few folks who, like you, are wanting to get a better idea of what the experience is like.

#causeascene Conference comes to Edinburgh!

Kim Crayton will be bringing the aclaimed #causeascene conference to Edinburgh on Sunday 22nd of July!

#causeascene Conference is designed to be safe space for members of underrepresented and marginalized communities in tech to tell their stories. To share what they’re working on, what they’ve experienced, their tech enabled hopes and dreams. This is an opportunity for these, often sidelined and silenced, individuals to use the platform to amplify their voices, uncensored, in a very authentic manner.

The CFP is open now as are ticket sales and donations!
