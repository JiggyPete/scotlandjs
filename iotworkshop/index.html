Edinburgh, June 2nd and 3rd 2016



IoT && Developer Happiness Workshop

June 4th: 10am - 5pm



Sponsored by:

Get started with a hands-on IoT workshop that will guide you through the basics of designing, prototyping, and deploying a connected device with Particle development hardware and the Losant IoT platform.

We will start by integrating with familiar developer technologies such as Travis, GitHub, and Slack to create an IoT device that provides useful functionality for common OSS developer tasks.

Attendees will leave with a solid understanding of how they might apply the concepts of IoT to improve their happiness and productivity at work and beyond.

Requirements:

  • A laptop
  • All other hardware will be provided.

Where and When?

This workshop will be held in the CodeClan office from 10:00am until 5pm.
