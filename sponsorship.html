Diversity Fund Contribution

In 2016 we put a huge effort into improving the diversity of the conferences. A large part of that was our diversity programme and also attracting speakers from underrepresented groups, both of which were a huge success. 30% of our attendees and 50% of our CFP speakers were women or non-binary people!

All contributions made will go towards:

  • Poviding free childcare!
  • Providing 30 free tickets to people from underrepresented groups for each conference!
  • Assisting speakers from underrepresented groups with travel and accommodation.
