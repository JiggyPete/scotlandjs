Childcare

We know how hard it is for parents to attend events like this while juggling childcare. To help with that we will have onsite childcare during the conference!

The law in Scotland around childcare is very strict to ensure safety and our childcare provider has already performed a full risk assessment.

If you would like to make use of the childcare facilities we need to know by June 1st 2018 for staffing purposes so please get in touch.
